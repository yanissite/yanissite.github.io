What this is

A few things we believe in.

🐢
It's not about speed.

Finishing a book in 3 days means nothing here. Reading it over 3 months and loving it? That's the whole point. Speed isn't a virtue — connection to the story is.
💛
No perfection required.
tap to read more ↓
Didn't read this week? Totally fine. Skipped a few chapters? We still want to hear your thoughts. This is a judgment-free zone — always.
🌱
Built for beginners.

If you're someone who wants to read more but never quite gets there — you're exactly who this is for. We pick accessible books, keep meetings relaxed, and welcome all levels.

Your pace is the right pace.
